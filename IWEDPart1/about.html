About Us

Our Story

Inspired by classic French and fresh ingredients, the bakery focuses on creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere for all our customers. Oui, Oui Bakery was originally created for people with gluten sensitivities, but since it was also created to bring people together, we have expanded our menu to include a wide range of options for everyone to enjoy. Our goal is to bring people together through fresh baked goods to make every visit feel special.
