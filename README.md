#Oui, Oui Bakery Website

##Description
This website is for Oui, Oui bakery, a french-inspired bakery and it is 
designed to showcase their high-quality baked goods, share their story and make it easier to 
attract customers. The website will reflect the bakery's brand personality. 
The website was also created to give the bakery an online presence and to provide important information about the bakery.

##Features
Navigation Menu for effortless browsing.
Homepage with banner and Introduction.
About page with photos of the team and the bakery's story.
Menu with the names of all the baked goods and beverages.
Gallery with photos of the bakery and the baked goods.
Embedded Google form and Google Maps.
